                          Hearty Broccoli Cheese Soup
                                  (Serves 6)
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
2 T olive oil
2 T flour
2 c. half and half
1 celery stalk, chopped
1 carrot, chopped
2 (1/2) c. broccoli florets
3 c. chicken broth
(1/4) c. cornstarch
1 t. salt
(1/2) t. pepper
(1/2) t. garlic powder
1 c. sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions:
In a large stock pot, heat oil over medium heat, add onion, and cook two minutes.  Whisk in flour and slowly add I cup broth, whisking continuously to avoid lumps.  Combine cornstarch and a little broth in a small container; shake until smooth.  Add to pot along with remaining ingredients except for cheese; cook seven minutes.  In a blender, place 2 cups of soup and blend until smooth.  Return to stockpot and add cheese.  Stir until melted.
