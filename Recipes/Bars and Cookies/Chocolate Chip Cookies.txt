                            Chocolate Chip Cookies
                                       
Ingredients:

1 cup brown sugar, packed
(1/2) cup margarine
2 eggs
1 (1/4) cups all-purpose flour
(1/2) cup cocoa
(1/2) tsp baking soda
(1/2) tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
(1/2) cup chopped nuts

Instructions: 

Place all ingredients in a mixing bowl except chips and nuts. Beat at medium speed until blended. Beat at high speed for 3 minutes. Fold in chips and nuts. Drop by spoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350Â° for 10 -12 minutes.
