                            Baked Mozzarella Bites
Ingredients:
8 oz. mozzarella cheese block
Ritz crackers
1 c. spaghetti sauce
Instructions: Heat oven to 325.  Cut cheese crosswise into 15 slices, then cut each slice in half.  Top 30 crackers with cheese; cover with remaining crackers.  Place in 15X10X1 inch pan.  Bake 8 minutes or until cheese begins to melt.  Meanwhile, heat spaghetti sauce.  Serve cracker sandwiches as dipper with sauce.
