                                Pineapple Cake
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 can crushed pineapple
2 t. vanilla
2 t. baking soda
Instructions: Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.  
Frosting: 
8 oz. cream cheese
(1/2) c. margarine
1 t. vanilla
1 (1/4) c. brown sugar
Mix together, and put over semi-warm cake.
