                             Peanut Butter Banana
                                       
Ingredients:

4 Tbsp PB Fit powder
1 scoop protein powder
(1/2) banana
(1/2) cup strawberries
1 cup almond milk

Instructions:

Step 1.
Cut and measure out ingredients.

Step 2.
Place ingredients in labeled plastic bags and freeze.

Step 3.
Blend 1 cup of liquid with ingredient pack.
