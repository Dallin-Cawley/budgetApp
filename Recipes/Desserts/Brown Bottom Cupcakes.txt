                             Brown Bottom Cupcakes
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1 unbeaten egg
1/3 c. sugar
1/8 t. salt
1 c. chocolate chips
1 (1/2) c. flour
1 c. sugar
(1/4) c. cocoa
1 t. soda
(1/2) t. salt
1 c. water
1/3 c. cooking oil
1 T. vinegar
1 t. vanilla
Instructions: Filling: combine in mixing bowl: cream cheese, egg, sugar, and salt.  Cream well.  Stir in chocolate chips. 
Sift together flour, sugar, cocoa, soda, and salt.  Add water, cooking oil, vinegar and vanilla.  Beat well.  Fill cupcake papers about 1/3 full with chocolate mixture then top each one with 1 t. of cream cheese mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.  Makes about 2 dozen cupcakes.
