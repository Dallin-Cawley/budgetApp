                                 Pumpkin Bars
                                       
Ingredients:

4 eggs
1 (2/3) cups sugar
1 cup oil
1-16 oz. can pumpkin
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda

Frosting:
8 oz. package cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla
(1/2) cup margarine
2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions: 

Beat together eggs, sugar, oil, and canned pumpkin until fluffy. Sift together flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda. Add the two mixes together. Spread in ungreased 15x10x1 inch jelly roll pan or oversized 10x14 inch cake pan. Bake at 350Â° for 25-30 minutes.  
Frosting: cream softened cream cheese, vanilla, and margarine. Gradually add powdered sugar.
