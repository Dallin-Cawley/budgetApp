TURKEY CHILI
1 RED .5 GREEN .5 YELLOW
1 tsp olive oil
1 1/2 lbs lean ground turkey
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 tsp. cumin
1 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp Himalayan salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (to taste; optional)
1 can black beans
1 can kidney beans
1 15 oz can all natural diced tomatoes, no added sugar
   1.         heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat
   2.         add turkey, onion, bell pepper and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally
   3.         add cumin, chili powder, salt, and cayenne pepper; cook, stirring constantly for 1 minute
   4.         add beans and tomatoes (don't drain). Bring to boil; reduce heat to low covered for 15-20
        minutes stirring occasionally.
