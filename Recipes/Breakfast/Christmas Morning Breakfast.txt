                          Christmas Morning Breakfast
Ingredients:
12 oz. low-fat breakfast sausage
3 slices white bread, torn into bite sized pieces
(1/2) shredded Monterey jack cheese
6 eggs
1 c. low-fat milk
(1/4) t. salt
1/8 t. pepper
(1/2) c. shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions: Brown sausage in skillet over medium heat, stirring to crumble.  Remove from heat, drain off any fat, and let cool.  Place bread in bottom on 2 quart baking dish lightly coated with cooking spray.  Layer the sausage over the bread, then top it with the Monterey jack cheese.  In medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.  Pour the mixture over the contents of the dish, then top with the cheddar cheese.  Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.  In the morning, heat oven to 325.  Remove the plastic wrap from dish and bake the casserole for 40-45 minutes or until cooked through.  Makes 6 servings.
