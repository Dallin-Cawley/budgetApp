                                 Cheese Quiche
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) c. cheese, grated
2 c. milk
(1/2) c. flour
4 eggs
(1/2) t. salt
(1/4) t. pepper
1 c. chopped vegetables (squash, onion, mushrooms, etc)
Instructions: Spread grated cheese in bottom of 10 inch pie plate.  Blend remaining ingredients and add to the cheese.  Bake 45 minutes to one hour at 350.  Serves 4-6. (This quiche makes its own crust)
