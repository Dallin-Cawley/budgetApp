                                Salted Nut Roll
Ingredients:
1-24 oz. dry roasted peanuts (unsalted)
1-12 oz. pkg. reeces peanut butter chips
1 cube margarine
1 can eagle brand milk
2 c. mini marshmallows
Instructions: Spread half jar of nuts on bottom of a 9X13 pan.  Melt together rest of ingredients over low heat.  Pour over nuts in pan.  Put rest of nuts on top of filling, pressing lightly.  Cool.  Enjoy!
