Chocolate Waffles

2 oz. Bitter Chocolate or 3 tbsp. Cocoa (I use cocoa powder)
1/2 c. Butter
2 eggs
1 c. Sugar
1 tsp. Salt
1 1/2 c. Flour
1/2 tsp. Baking powder
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 c. Milk

Melt chocolate and butter together.  In separate bowl, beat eggs slightly and add sugar.  Gradually add chocolate mixture to eggs and sugar.  Add milk and vanilla.  Beat until light chocolate.  In separate bowl, mix flour, salt, and baking powder.  Gradually stir flour mixture into chocolate mixture.  Let sit in refrigerator for approximately 1 hour.  Cook in waffle maker.  Serve hot, topped with vanilla ice cream.
