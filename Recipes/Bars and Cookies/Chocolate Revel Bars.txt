                             Chocolate Revel Bars
                                       
Ingredients:

1 cup butter/margarine
3 cups brown sugar
3 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
3-3 (1/2) cups flour
1 (1/2) tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
4-4 (1/2) cups oatmeal

Filling:
12 oz. package chocolate chips
15 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
2 Tbsp butter/margarine
2 tsp vanilla

Instructions: 

Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cream sugar and butter/margarine. Add eggs and vanilla. Then stir in remaining ingredients.  

Filling: Melt filling ingredients together in microwave (1-2 min). Beat until smooth. Spread (2/3) of dough in 12x17x1 inch jelly roll pan (greased or sprayed with non-stick coating). Cover with chocolate filling. Dot with remaining dough. Bake 25-30 minutes. Cool before cutting.
