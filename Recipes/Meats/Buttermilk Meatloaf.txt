                              Buttermilk Meatloaf
Ingredients:
2/3 c. dry bread crumbs
1 c. buttermilk
(1/2) t. dried thyme
(1/4) t. dried oregano
2 eggs, beaten
2 t. Worcestershire sauce
1 t. salt
(1/2) t. freshly ground pepper
1 lb. bulk breakfast sausage or ground pork
(1/4) c. minced onion
1 sm. grated carrot
3 T. brown sugar
(1/4) c. ketchup
1 T. Dijon mustard
Instructions: Preheat oven to 350.  Grease a 9X13 baking pan.  Soak crumbs in buttermilk for 5 minutes.  Stir in thyme, oregano, and eggs, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.  Add meats, onion, and carrot.  Mix well.  Shape into loaf, and place in prepared pan.  Flatten top.  Mix brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard, and pour half of sauce over meatloaf.  Bake 30 minutes.  Brush with remaining sauce, and bake another 30 minutes.  Remove from oven and let rest 10 minutes before slicing.  Makes 6-8 servings.
