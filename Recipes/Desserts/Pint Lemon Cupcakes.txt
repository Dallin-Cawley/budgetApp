                              Pint Lemon Cupcakes
Ingredients:
Cupcakes:
2 sticks butter, softened
2 c. granulated sugar
4 eggs
2 (3/4) c. all-purpose flour
(1/2) t. baking soda
(1/2) t. salt
1 c. milk
2 T. lemon zest
2 T. freshly squeezed lemon juice
Powdered sugar
Frosting:
4 c. powdered sugar
(1/2) c. butter, softened
2 T. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 t. grated lemon zest
2 T. milk
Pink gel tint
Instructions: Bake at 350 for 15-18 minutes.
