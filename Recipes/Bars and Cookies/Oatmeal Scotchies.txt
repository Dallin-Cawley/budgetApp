                               Oatmeal Scotchies
                                       
Ingredients:

1 (1/4) cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
(1/2) tsp salt
(1/2) tsp cinnamon
1 cup butter, softened
(3/4) cup granulated sugar
(3/4) cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla or grated peel of 1 orange
3 cups quick or old fashioned oats
1 (2/3) cups butterscotch morsels

Instructions: 

Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla in large mixer bowl. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in oats and morsels. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 375Â° for 7-8 minutes. Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
