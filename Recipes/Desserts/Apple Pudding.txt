                                 Apple Pudding
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/3 c. shortening or margarine
1 egg
4 c. grated apples
1 c. flour
1 t. soda
(1/2) t. salt
(1/2) t. cinnamon
1/3 t. nutmeg
(1/2) c. nuts
Sauce:
2 c. boiling water
(1/2) c. brown sugar
(1/2) c. white sugar
Salt
1 t. vanilla
1/8 square butter
Instructions: Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
