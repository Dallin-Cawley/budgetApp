                               Favorite Cobbler
Ingredients:
(1/2) c. butter
1 c. flour
1 c. sugar
1 t. baking powder
(1/4) t. salt
1 c. milk
2 c. canned fruit, well drained
Instructions: Melt butter in a 2 qt. casserole dish.  Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into bowl.  Stir in milk.  Pour batter over melted butter.  Don't stir.  Place well drained fruit on top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes until batter covers fruit and crust is brown.
