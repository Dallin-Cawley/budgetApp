                        Blue Cheese and Artichoke Salad
Ingredients:
(1/2) t. salt
1 t. sugar
(1/4) t. pepper
1 t. ground dry mustard
2 T. red wine vinegar
1/3 c. olive oil
4 t. lemon juice
2 heads chopped romaine
1 can artichoke hearts 
1/3-1/2 C. crumbled blue cheese

Instructions:  Combine first 7 ingredients for dressing.  Pour over chopped romaine, artichoke hearts and blue cheese.
