                             Pineapple Cream Cake
Ingredients:
Yellow cake mix
1-8 oz. cream cheese
(1/2) c. milk
2 c. milk
1-3 oz. vanilla instant pudding
2-16 oz. cans crushed pineapple
1 c. whipping cream
1-8 oz. cool whip
Coconut
Instructions: Prepare original recipe on back of cake mix.  Bake in sheet cake pan for 20-25 minutes.  Cool.  Mix cream cheese with (1/2) c. milk.  Beat together and add 2 c. milk and vanilla instant pudding.  Beat until mixed well and let set up a little.  Spread over cake.  Chill 30 minutes.  Drain cans of crushed pineapple.  Spread over pudding.  Whip whipping cream then fold in cool whip.  Garnish with coconut.
