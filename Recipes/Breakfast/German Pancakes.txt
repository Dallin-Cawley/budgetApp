                                German Pancakes
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 c. milk
1 c. all purpose flour
(1/4) t. salt
4 T. butter
Instructions: In a bowl, combine eggs, milk, flour, and salt.  Beat until fluffy and smooth.  Put 2 T. of butter in each pie pan.  Melt as the oven preheats to 450 degrees.  Pour half of batter into each pan.  Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
