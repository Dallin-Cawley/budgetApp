                               Classic Meat Loaf
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) lbs. ground beef
8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 c. quick cooking rolled oats
1 T. instant minced onion
1/8 t. salt
(1/4) t. pepper
Instructions: Combine all ingredients.  Pat into 9X5X20 loaf dish.  Cook 8-10 minutes full power in microwave.  
Sauce: 
1/3 c. ketchup
1 t. Worcestershire sauce
1 T. dark brown sugar
1 T. prepared mustard
Blend ingredients well.  Pour over meat loaf halfway through cooking time.  Make 6-8 servings.
