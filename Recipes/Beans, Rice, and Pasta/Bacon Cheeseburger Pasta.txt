                           Bacon Cheeseburger Pasta
                                       
Ingredients:

1 lb. pasta
2 lb. ground beef
12 bacon strips, diced
2 cans condensed tomato soup
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
Prepared yellow mustard and/or barbeque sauce

Instructions:

Cook beef in a large skillet over medium heat until no longer pink; drain and set aside. In the same skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove and drain on paper towels. Discard drippings.
Cook pasta according to package. Drain and add to the same skillet along with remaining ingredients, except cheese. Heat through. Top with cheese, cover and cook until cheese is melted. Serve with prepared mustard or barbeque sauce.
Serves 8-10.
