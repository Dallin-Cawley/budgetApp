Famous Golden Cornbread
                                       
Ingredients:

1.25 cups all-purpose flour
.75 cup cornmeal
2 teaspoons baking powder
.33 cup sugar
.25 teaspoon salt
1.25 cups whole milk
.25 cup shortening
1 egg

Instructions: 

Preheat oven to 400Â°. Combine the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a medium bowl. Add the milk, shortening, and egg and mix only until all the ingredients are well combined. Pour into a greased 8x8 pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until top is golden brown. Cool slightly before cutting.  
Honey Butter:
Whip (1/2) c. softened butter with 1/3 c. honey until smooth and fluffy.
