                               Irish Soda Bread
Ingredients:
4 c. flour (2 white, 2 wheat)
(1/2) c. sugar
1 t. salt
2 c. raisins
1 (1/2) c. buttermilk
1 egg
2 t. baking powder
(1/2) c. butter
1 t. soda
Instructions:  Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Mix dry ingredients; cut in butter.  Add raisins.  Mix together egg and buttermilk.  Add to dry ingredients.  Bake for 45-50 minutes.
