                              Chex Lemon Buddies
                                       
9 c. Chex rice cereal
1 (1/4) white vanilla baking chips
(1/4) c. butter or margarine
4 t. grated lemon peel
2 T. fresh lemon juice
2 c. powdered sugar

Microwave chips, butter, lemon peel, and juice uncovered on high for one minute.  Stir.  Microwave about 30 seconds longer until mixture can be stirred smooth.  Pour mixture over cereal, stirring until evenly coated.  Pour into 2 gallon storage plastic bag.  Add powdered sugar and seal bag.  Gently shake until well coated.  Spread onto waxed paper or foil to cool.
