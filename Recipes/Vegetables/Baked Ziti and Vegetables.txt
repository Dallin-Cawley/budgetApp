                           Baked Ziti and Vegetables
Ingredients:
16 oz. pkg. ziti or penne macaroni
2 med. Size green peppers
2 med. Sized carrots
2 med. Stalks of celery
1 med. Onion
1 T. salad oil
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
3 c. spicy-hot vegetable juice (bottle)
1 T. sugar
1 (1/2) t. salt
(1/2) t. dried oregano leaves
8 oz. shredded reduced fat mozzarella cheese (2 c.)
2 T. grated parmesan cheese
Instructions: Prepare ziti as label directs.  Meanwhile, cut green peppers, carrots, celery, and onion into (1/2) inch pieces.  In nonstick 12 inch skillet over medium-high heat, in hot salad oil, cook veggies until lightly browned.  Stir in (1/4) c. water; continue cooking over medium heat until veggies are tender crisp.  Preheat oven to 375.  Drain ziti ; set aside.  To same saucepot, add cooked veggies; stir in crushed tomatoes, vegetable juice, sugar, salt, and oregano; over high heat, heat to boiling.  Remove saucepot from heat; stir in cooked ziti.  Reserve one c. shredded mozzarella for topping.  Into ziti mixture in saucepot, stir parmesan and remaining mozzarella cheese.  Spoon mixture into shallow 4 quart casserole; sprinkle with reserved mozzarella cheese. Cover casserole and bake 30 minutes or until cheese melts and mixture is hot and bubbly.  Serves 6.
