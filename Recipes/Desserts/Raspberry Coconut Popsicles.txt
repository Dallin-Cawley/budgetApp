                          Raspberry Coconut Popsicles
Ingredients:
13.5 oz. can light coconut milk
4 c. fresh raspberries
(1/2) c. honey
(1/2) c. sugar
2 limes, zested and juiced
(1/4) c. shredded coconut
10-12 popsicle sticks

Instructions: Blend all ingredients except coconut until smooth.  Stir in coconut.  Pour into molds, freeze for 2 hours.  Once mixture has partially frozen, insert sticks.  Freeze for additional 2 hours.
