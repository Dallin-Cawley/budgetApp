                                Hot Fudge Sauce
Ingredients:
(3/4) c. semisweet chocolate pieces
(1/4) c. butter
2/3 c. sugar
5 oz. can evaporated milk (2/3 c.)
Instructions: In small heavy saucepan, melt the chocolate and butter.  Add the sugar; gradually stir in the evaporated milk.  Bring mixture to boiling; reduce heat.  Boil gently over low heat for 8 minutes, stirring frequently.  Remove pan from heat.  Cool slightly.  Serve warm over ice cream.  Cover and chill any leftovers.  Makes 1 (1/2) cups.
