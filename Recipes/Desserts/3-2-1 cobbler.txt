                                 3-2-1 Cobbler
Ingredients:
3 cans fruit, 2 cans one flavor and 1 of another
2 cake mixes, 1 white, one yellow
1 can lemon lime soda
3 T. sugar
2 t. cinnamon
Instructions: Pour fruit into foil lined 14 inch oven.  Drain most of the fluid if you are doing peaches.  Pour dry cake mixes over the top of the fruit then pour soda on top.  Stir gently with fork.  Be careful not to stir into fruit.  Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top.  Cover and bake about 45 minutes with about 11 coals on the bottom and 14 on top.
