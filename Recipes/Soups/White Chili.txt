                                  White Chili
Ingredients:
5 cans great northern beans (2 (1/2) c. dry)
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 t. cumin
4 c. cooked diced chicken
4 c. chicken broth
2 medium onion, chopped
2-4 oz. cans diced green chilies
1 (1/2) t. oregano
(1/4) t. cayenne pepper
(1/4) t. white pepper
16 oz. light sour cream (add before serving)
Instructions: Combine beans, garlic, broth, and onions.  Bring to a boil and simmer 15 minutes.  Add rest of ingredients and simmer one hour.  Can add chopped chives.  May add sour cream at end.
