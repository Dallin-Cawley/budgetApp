                            Raisin Oatmeal Classics
                                       
Ingredients:

(3/4) cup butter or margarine
1 cup packed brown sugar
(1/2) cup granulated sugar
(1/4) cup milk
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
(1/2) tsp cinnamon
(1/2) tsp baking soda
(1/2) tsp salt
3 cups old fashioned oats
1 cup sun-maid raisins

Instructions: 

Heat oven to 350Â°. Beat butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, milk, egg, and vanilla until light and fluffy. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt. Add butter mixture and mix well. Stir in oats and raisins. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets, cool on wire racks.
