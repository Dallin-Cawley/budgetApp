                         Twist of Lime Zucchini Bread
Ingredients:
4 eggs
(1/2) c. oil
2 c. sugar
2 c. grated zucchini
1 T. vanilla
3 c. flour
(1/2) t. salt
1 (1/2) t. baking powder
1 t. soda
3 t. cinnamon
(1/2) t. ground ginger
Lime zest and juice

Instructions: Beat eggs.  Add oil, sugar, zucchini, and vanilla.  Mix well.  Add flour, salt, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, and ginger; mix.  Zest peel of 1 lime into the bowl.  Cut the lime in half and squeeze juice into the bowl.  Mix all very well.  Pour into 2 greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes.
