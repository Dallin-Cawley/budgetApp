                            Sour Cream Coffee Cake
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) c. sugar
(1/2) c. margarine or butter, softened
1 (1/2) t. vanilla
3 eggs
3 c. all purpose or whole wheat flour
1 (1/2) t. baking powder
1 (1/2) t. baking soda
(3/4) t. salt
1 (1/2) c. sour cream
Brown Sugar Filling:
Mix (1/2) c. packed brown sugar, (1/2) c. finely chopped nuts, and 1 (1/2) t. ground cinnamon.  
Instructions: Heat oven to 325 degrees.  Grease tube pan, 10 X 4 inches or 12 cup bunt pan.  Prepare the filling.  Beat sugar, margarine, vanilla, and eggs on medium speed, scraping the bowl occasionally, 2 minutes.  Beat in flour, baking powder, soda, and salt alternately with sour cream on low speed.  Spread 1/3 of the batter (about 2 cups) in pan; sprinkle with 1/3 of the filling.  Repeat two times.  Bake until wooden pick inserted near the center comes out clean, 1 hour.  Cool about 20 minutes, remove from pan, and drizzle with glaze.  
Glaze:  Mix (1/2) c. powdered sugar, (1/4) t. vanilla, and 1-2 t. milk until smooth.
