                       Chocolate Cherry Buttermilk Shake
Ingredients:
1 c. chocolate ice cream
1 c. frozen, pitted dark cherries
1 c. buttermilk
6 coarsely chopped chocolate sandwich cookies
Instructions: In a blender, combine the chocolate ice cream, frozen cherries, and buttermilk.  Blend until smooth.  Add the cookie sandwiches and stir to combine.  Serve immediately.
