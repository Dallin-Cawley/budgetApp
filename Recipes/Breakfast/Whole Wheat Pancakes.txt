                             Whole Wheat Pancakes
Ingredients:
1 c. milk
(3/4) c. wheat
(1/4) c. sugar
2 eggs
(1/4) c. melted butter
1 t. baking soda
2 t. baking powder
(1/4) t. salt
Instructions: Blend milk and wheat in blender for 4 minutes at high speed.  Add remaining ingredients and mix for 3 minutes.  If you like thicker batter, add some extra flour or cut down on the milk.
