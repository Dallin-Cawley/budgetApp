                                  Butterbeer 
Ingredients:
1 quart vanilla ice cream
(1/4) c. butterscotch syrup
32 oz. of favorite cream soda
Instructions: Place 4 (16 oz.) glasses in freezer and chill for at least 20 minutes.  Mix ice cream, syrup, and cream soda in blender until combined.  Divide between the frosted glasses.  Top each glass with whipped cream if desired.
