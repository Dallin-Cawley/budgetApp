                                Root Beer Cake
Ingredients:
Mix together one white cake mix
1 (1/4) c. root beer
3 egg whites
(1/4) c. oil
Topping: 
1 dream whip pkg. 
(1/2) c. root beer
Instructions: Cook in 9X13 pan for 30 minutes at 350.  Mix topping ingredients until fluffy and top the cooled down cake.
