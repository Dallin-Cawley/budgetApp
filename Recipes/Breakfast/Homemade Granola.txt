                               Homemade Granola
Ingredients:
(1/2) c. shredded coconut
4 c. old fashioned oats
(1/4) c. toasted wheat germ
(1/4) c. unsalted sunflower seeds
1 c. coarsely chopped almonds
1 T. sesame seeds
(1/2) t. ground cinnamon
(1/4) t. freshly grated nutmeg
(1/2) c. honey
8 T. unsalted butter, melted
(1/2) c. golden raisins
Instructions: Heat oven to 350.  Line a baking sheet with parchment, and spread the shredded coconut on top.  Bake until toasted.  Transfer to wire rack to cool.  Decrease oven temperature to 300.  Line two baking sheets with parchment; set aside.  In large bowl, toss together oats, wheat germ, sunflower seeds, almonds, sesame seeds, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Set aside.  In small bowl, stir together honey and butter; pour over oat mixture.  Stir well.  Spread onto sheets.  Bake until golden, about 25 minutes.  Transfer to wire racks to cool.  Break up granola; sprinkle with raisins and toasted coconut.
