                                 Red Hot Jelly
                                       
2 c. apple juice
4 c. white sugar
(1/2) t. margarine
1/3 c. red cinnamon candies
1 pouch (6 oz.) liquid pectin

Wash and sterilize canning jars and lids.  In large heavy saucepan, combine juice and sugar.  Stir well.  Add margarine and candies.  Bring to a rolling coil over high heat, stirring constantly.  Stir in liquid pectin.  Return to a rolling boil over high heat, stirring constantly, and boil 1 minute.  Remove from heat.  Skim foam from top of mixture with large metal spoon.  Fill sterilized jars to within (1/4) inch of tops.  Wipe jar rims and seal jars.
