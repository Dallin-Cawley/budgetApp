                                Oatmeal Waffles
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) c. all purpose flour
(1/2) c. quick-cooking rolled oats
(1/2) T. baking powder
(1/2) t. cinnamon
1 t. baking soda
(1/4) t. salt
2 eggs, separated
1 (1/2) c. buttermilk
6 T. melted butter
2 T. brown sugar
Instructions:  In a large mixing bowl, stir together flour, oats, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.  In a small mixing bowl, stir together egg yolks, milk, butter, and brown sugar.  Add to flour mixture.  Stir just until blended.  Fold in beaten egg whites.
