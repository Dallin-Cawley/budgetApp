All-Season Quick Loaves
                                       
Ingredients:

3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp each, salt and baking soda
(1/2) tsp baking powder
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup chopped nuts
2 cups prepared fruit or vegetable
3 eggs
1 (1/2) cups sugar
1 cup salad oil
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions: 

In a large bowl, stir together flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and nuts. Set aside. Prepare fruit or vegetable of your choice, set aside. In another bowl, lightly beat eggs. Add sugar and oil and stir until blended. Stir in vanilla and prepared fruit or vegetable. Add flour mixture all at once and stir until evenly moistened. Grease and flour two 4 (1/2) x 8 (1/2) loaf pans. Bake at 350ยบ for 50-60 minutes.
