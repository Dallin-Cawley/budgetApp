                            Raspberry Cream Trifle
Ingredients:
1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk, chilled
1 c. cold water
1 t. almond extract
1 pkg. (3.4 oz.) vanilla instant pudding
2 c. heacy whipping cream, whipped
1 angel food cake (7 inches), cut into 1 inch cubes
2 T. seedless raspberry jam
2 c. fresh raspberries
Chocolate curls and fresh mint
Instructions: In large mixing bowl, beat the milk, water, and extract until blended.  Add pudding mix; whisk for 2 minutes.  Let stand for 2 minutes or until soft-set.  Cover and chill until mixture is partially set.  Fold in the whipped cream.  Place half of the cake cubes in a 2 qt. glass serving bowl.  Top with half of the cream mixture.  Carefully spread with jam.  Sprinkle with 1 c. raspberries.  Layer with remaining cake cubes, cream mixture and raspberries.  Garnish with chocolate curls and mint.  Makes about 14-16 servings.
