                             Easy Black Bean Soup
Ingredients:
3 T. olive oil
1 med. onion, chopped
1 T. gound cumin
2-3 cloves garlic
2 (14.5 oz.) cans black beans
2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
(1/4)-1/2 c. salsa
Salt and pepper
(1/4) c. cilantro, chopped

Instructions:
Saut√© onion in olive oil.  When onion becomes translucent, add cumin.  Cook 30 seconds, then add garlic and cook for another 30 to 60 seconds.  Add 1 can black beans and 2 cups broth.  Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally.  Turn off heat.  Using a hand blender, blend the ingredients in the pot, or transfer to a blender.  Add the second can of beans to the blended ingredients and bring to a simmer.  Serve with cilantro for garnish.
