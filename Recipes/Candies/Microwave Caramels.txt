                              Microwave Caramels
Ingredients:
2 c. white sugar
1 c. white karo syrup
1 c. real butter
I can sweetened condensed milk
Spoonful of vanilla
Instructions: Combine in a large bowl and stir well.  Place in microwave (I place a piece of waxed paper over the top) and cook on high for 5 minutes.  Remove from microwave and add sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.  Place back in microwave and cook on high for 4 minutes.  Remove and stir.  Place back in microwave and cook for 4 minutes.  Remove and stir.  Place back in microwave and cook for 4 minutes more, always on high.  (this is a total cooking time of 17 minutes)  Remove from microwave, stir well, and pour into a 9X13 pan.  Cool and cut into 1 inch squares.  Wrap in plastic wrap if desired.
