                              Amish Baked Oatmeal
Ingredients:
3 c. oats
(3/4) c. sugar
2 t. baking powder
1 pinch salt
2 eggs
(1/4) c. butter, melted
(1/2) canned milk
2 t. vanilla
Instructions: Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into greased 8.5X11 inch baking dish.  Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.  (may stir oatmeal halfway through baking)  Dish into bowls, pour warmed milk over the top.  Can top with fresh fruit.
