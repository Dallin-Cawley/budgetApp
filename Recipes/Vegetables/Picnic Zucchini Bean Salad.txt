                        Garden Full of Goodness Lasagna
Ingredients:
2 c. thinly sliced zucchini
2 c. thinly sliced yellow squash
1 c. thinly sliced carrot
1 c. thinly sliced fresh mushrooms
1 med. Onion, diced
(1/2) green bell pepper, diced
(1/2) red bell pepper, diced
14 oz. can crushed tomatoes
8 oz. can tomato sauce
6 oz. can tomato paste
(1/4) c. sliced fresh basil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 t. salt
(1/2) t. ground black pepper
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
(3/4) c. sm. curd cottage cheese
1 large egg, lightly beaten
8 oven-ready in no cook in lasagna noodles
12 slices provolone cheese, thinly sliced
2 c. shredded mozzarella
Instructions: Combine zucchini, squash, carrot, mushrooms, onion, and pepper with water to cover.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat; reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes or until veggies are tender.  Drain well and return to pot.  Preheat oven to 350.  Combine crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, garlic, salt, and pepper in medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes.  In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, cottage cheese, and eggs. Stir together.  Spread 1/3 of sauce evenly over bottom of a 9X13 baking dish.  Place 4 uncooked lasagna noodles on top of sauce.  Do not overlap noodles.  Spread (1/2) of cream cheese mixture over noodles.  Cover cheese mixture with (1/2) the vegetable mixture, more sauce, and top evenly with 6 slices of provolone cheese and 1 (1/2) c. mozzarella.  Repeat layers with 4 noodles, rest of cream cheese mixture, vegetables, sauce, and remaining cheeses.  Place in oven for 35 minutes or until lasagna is hot and bubbling.
