                               Noelle's Chili
Ingredients:
2 cans each of kidney, pinto, and black beans
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 can diced green chilies
1 can tomato paste
1 can corn
2 bell peppers, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 lbs. ground beef
(1/2) bunch cilantro
Instructions: Brown beef, onion, and pepper.  Add rest.  Chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper, to taste.    I also add a can of crushed tomatoes.
