                          Magic Peanut Butter Middles
                                       
Ingredients:

1 (1/2) cups flour
(1/2) cup unsweetened baking cocoa
(1/2) tsp baking soda
(1/2) cup sugar
(1/2) cup packed brown sugar
(1/2) cup margarine, softened
(1/4) cup creamy peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg
(3/4) cup powdered sugar
(3/4) cup creamy peanut butter
Additional sugar
Chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions: 

Mix flour, baking cocoa, and baking soda in bowl. Cream sugar, brown sugar, margarine, and (1/4) cup peanut butter in mixer bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in vanilla and egg. Stir in flour mixture. Set dough aside. Mix powdered sugar, and (3/4) cup peanut butter in a small bowl, stirring until creamy. Roll into 1 inch balls. Envelope with about 1 Tbsp dough to cover completely. Arrange 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten with bottom of glass dipped in sugar. Bake at 375Â° for 7-9 minutes or until set and slightly cracked. Optional: two chocolate chips can be placed inside the peanut butter balls for an extra surprise.
