                              Red Marinara Sauce
                                       
Ingredients:

(1/2) cup olive oil
2 garlic cloves, pressed
2 Tbsp oregano leaves (dried)
2 Tbsp basil leaves (dried)
3-28 oz. cans crushed tomatoes
1 Tbsp sugar

Instructions: 

In large kettle, combine olive oil and garlic. Heat a little. Add all at once the oregano and basil. Heat to hot, but do not burn. Quickly add tomatoes and sugar. Heat thoroughly. Let boil, but not more than 5-10 minutes.
