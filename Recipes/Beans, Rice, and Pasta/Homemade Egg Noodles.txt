                             Homemade Egg Noodles
                                       
Ingredients:

1 egg, slightly beaten
(1/4) tsp salt
(2/3) cup flour

Instructions: 

Knead all ingredients together. Cover and let stand for (1/2) hour or so. Roll out very thing. Cut in strips. Add to boiling liquid and cook. Can also be dried and used like regular egg noodles.
