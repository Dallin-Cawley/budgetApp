                               Spicy Oven Fries
Ingredients:
1 T. parmesan cheese
(1/2) t. salt 
1/8 t. oregano
1/8 t. onion salt
1/8/ t. pepper
1/8 t. chili powder
Dash of cayenne pepper
Olive oil
Sliced potatoes

Instructions:
Mix together seasoning ingredients.  Toss potatoes with olive oil.  Sprinkle seasoning over potatoes and toss to coat evenly.  Spreadin even layer on lined cookie sheet.  Bake at 475 for 7 minutes.  Turn over and bake another 7 minutes.
