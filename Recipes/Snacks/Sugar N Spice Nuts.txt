                              Sugar N Spice Nuts
                                       
3 c. lightly salted mixed nuts
1 egg white
1 T. orange juice
2/3 c. sugar
1 T. grated orange peel
1 t. ground cinnamon
(1/2) t. ground ginger
(1/2) t. ground allspice

Place nuts in large bowl.  In a small bowl, beat egg white and orange juice with a fork until foamy.  Add sugar, orange peel, cinnamon, ginger, and allspice; mix well.  Pour over nuts and stir to coat.  Spread into an ungreased 15X10 inch baking pan.  Bake at 275 stirring every 15 minutes for 45 minutes or until nuts are crisp and lightly browned.  Cool completely.  Yields 4 cups.
