Shirley Togleman's Refrigerator Hot Rolls

Ingredients:
1.5 T. yeast
.5 cup warm water
.5 t. sugar
2 cup warm water
.5 cup sugar
.5 cup shortening
2 t. salt
1 egg
3 cup flour

Instructions:
Dissolve bracket and let sit til foamy.  Use a mixer to combine the above ingredients then add the yeast mixture.  Start working 3-4 more cups of flour.  Bake at 400 for 15-20 minutes.
