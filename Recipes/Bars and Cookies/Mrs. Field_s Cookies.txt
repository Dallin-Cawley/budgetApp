                            Mrs. Field's Cookies
                                       
Ingredients:

1 lb. butter
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
4 cups flour
5 cups quick oats
1-16 oz. package chocolate chips
3 cups chopped nuts
1-5.1 oz. instant vanilla pudding (large box)

Instructions: 

Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Gradually add remaining dry ingredients. Mix well. Shape into golf ball size and place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Press slightly to flatten. Bake at 400Â° for 6 minutes only-no longer. Do not remove from pan until cool.
