                            Spicy Cabbage-Beef Soup
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lg. onion
5 cups chopped cabbage (bite-size)
2 cans (16 oz.) red kidney beans
3 cans (8oz.) tomato sauce
2 cups water
1 green bell pepper, chopped
(3/4) c. picante sauce
4 beef bouillon cubes
1 (1/2) t. cumin
(1/2) t. salt
(1/4) t. pepper

Instructions:
Brown ground beef and onion.  Combine with remaining ingredients in crock pot.  Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours or High 3-4 hours.  Makes 10-12 servings.
