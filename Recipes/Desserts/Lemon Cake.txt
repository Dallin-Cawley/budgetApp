                                  Lemon Cake
Ingredients:
1 pkg. yellow cake mix
(3/4) c. oil
(3/4) c. water
1 (3 oz. pkg.) lemon jello
4 eggs
Instructions: Add all ingredients to cake mix and beat 4 minutes.  Pour into greased and floured 9X13 pan.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.  Take out and let stand 3 minutes.  Prick the top all over with large fork and pour the glaze over it while it is still hot.  
Glaze:
 Juice of one lemon
Juice of one orange
2 c. powdered sugar
Rind of one lemon
