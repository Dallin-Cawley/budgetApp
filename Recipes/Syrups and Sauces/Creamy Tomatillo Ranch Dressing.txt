                        Creamy Tomatillo Ranch Dressing
Ingredients:
1 oz. pkg. ranch dressing mix
1 c. buttermilk
1 c. mayo
1 large or 2 sm. tomatillos
1 clove garlic, minced
(1/2) bunch cilantro, chopped
(1/2) t. lime juice
(1/2) -1 sm. jalapeno, seeds removed
Instructions: Blend all ingredients together and store in fridge to let flavors meld.  Dressing tends to get hotter the longer it sits.
