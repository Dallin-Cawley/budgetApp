                              Pumpkin Spice Cake
Ingredients:
Spice cake mix
Sm. can of pumpkin
(1/2) c. water
2 eggs
Instructions: Bake in a 12X17 inch pan for 22-24 minutes at 350.  While hot, sprinkle with (1/2) c. brown sugar.  Cool completely. Spread with 12 oz. cool whip.  Drizzle with chocolate and caramel sauce.
