                             Beef and Barley Soup
Ingredients:
1 T. cooking oil
2 lbs. beef short ribs
2 medium onions, chopped
3 large carrots, sliced
3 celery stalks, sliced
28 oz. can whole tomatoes with liquid, chopped
2 qts. Water
4 chicken bouillon cubes
1/3 c. medium pearl barley
Instructions: In a large dutch oven or kettle, heat oil over medium high.  Brown beef.  Add onions, carrots, celery, tomatoes, water, and bouillon; bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer for about 2 hours or until beef is tender.  Add barley; simmer another 50-60 minutes or until the barley is done.  Makes 10-12 servings.
