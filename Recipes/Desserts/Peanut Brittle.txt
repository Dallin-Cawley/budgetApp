                                Peanut Brittle
Ingredients:
1 c. karo syrup
(1/2) c. water
2 c. sugar
2 c. peanuts
1 T. butter
1 t. vanilla
2 t. soda
Instructions: Boil until turns light brown.  Add peanuts.  Cool one minute and remove from stove.  Add butter, vanilla, and soda.  Stir vigorously.  Put into heavy buttered pan.  Don't stir just move around.  Cool thoroughly and break into pieces.
