                                Pumpkin Muffins
Ingredients:
1 c. white sugar
(1/2) c. brown sugar
(1/2) c. margarine or butter
15 oz. pumpkin
1 egg
1 t. vanilla
1 (3/4) c. flour
2 t. baking powder
(1/2) t. salt
(1/2) t. cinnamon
1 c. chocolate chips
Instructions: Cream together sugars and butter.  Then add pumpkin, eggs, and vanilla.  Beat well.  Stir in flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and chocolate chips.  Bake for 15 minutes at 400 degrees.
