                           Marshmallow Caramel Sauce
                                       
1 (1/2) c. brown sugar, firmly packed
2/3 c. light corn syrup
1/3 c. water
1/3 c. butter or margarine
(1/2) c. milk
6 large marshmallows, diced

Combine sugar, corn syrup, and water in 1 qt. casserole.  Cook in microwave oven on full power for 7-9 minutes or until soft ball stage is reached.  Stir occasionally during cooking.  Beat in remaining ingredients.  Chill.
