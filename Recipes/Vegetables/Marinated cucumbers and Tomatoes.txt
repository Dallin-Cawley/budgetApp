MARINATED CUCUMBERS AND TOMATOES
INGREDIENTS
SERVINGS 6-8
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3medium cucumbers, peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1medium onion, sliced and separated into rings
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[1]⁄2cup vinegar
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[1]⁄4cup sugar
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2teaspoons salt
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1teaspoon fresh coarse ground black pepper
   * --------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[1]⁄4cup oil
   * 1teaspoon chopped fresh dill (optional)
DIRECTIONS
   1.          Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
   2.          Toss well to mix.
   3.          Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
