                              Rice Krispies Candy
Ingredients:
1 c. karo syrup
(1/2) c. sugar
1 c. peanut butter
6 c. rice krispies
1 c. chococlate chips
1 c. peanut butter or butterscotch chips
Instructions: Melt the karo, sugar, and peanut butter together in the microwave.  Add peanut butter and cereal.  Pat flat into a rectangular pan.  Melt chocolate chips and other chips in microwave about 90 seconds.  Frost krispies mix with melted chips and chill until set.  Cut and enjoy!
