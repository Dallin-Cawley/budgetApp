Beef and Broccoli Stir-fry

Ingredients:

1 lb. beef flank steak, sliced thinly cross grain

2 medium onions, sliced

2 lbs. broccoli, cut into bite sized pieces

2 cloves garlic

Instructions:
 Sprinkle 1 t. salt, 1 t. cornstarch, and 2 t. soy
sauce over sliced meat. Cook garlic in hot oil, remove. Cook
meat, remove. Cook broccoli, sliced onions, and sauce (1 t.
sugar, 1 t. salt, and 2 t. soy sauce) Combine all and serve
over rice.
