                    Old-Fashioned Buttermilk Fudge Brownies
                                       
Ingredients:

(1/2) cup butter
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
2 cups granulated sugar
3 eggs
1 Tbsp vanilla
(1/2) cup buttermilk
1 (1/2) cups flour, plus 2 Tbsp flour, divided
1 (1/2) cups semisweet chocolate chips
2 Tbsp powdered sugar
(1/4)-(1/2) tsp salt

Instructions: 

Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large microwaveable bowl, combine butter and chocolate. Melt butter and chocolate in microwave on high at 1 minutes intervals, stirring in between. Do not let mixture sizzle. When mixture is completely melted and smooth, immediately stir in sugar; continue stirring until smooth. Add eggs, one at a time. Stir in vanilla and buttermilk. Add 1 (1/2) cups flour and salt. Top chocolate chips with remaining 2 Tbsp flour in a small bowl; add to batter. Pour into greased 9x13 inch baking dish and bake for 28-33 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center has just a few fudgy crumbs. Let cool for 30 minutes. Dust with powdered sugar.
