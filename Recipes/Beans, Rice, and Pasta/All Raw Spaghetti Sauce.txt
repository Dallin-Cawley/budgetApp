                            All Raw Spaghetti Sauce
                                       
Ingredients:

6 large tomatoes
5 sun dried tomatoes
2 garlic cloves
(1/2) bunch fresh basil
2 Tbsp oregano
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
(1/4) cup chopped onion
(1/2) cup cold-pressed olive oil
(1/4) cup lemon juice
4 dates, pitted
1 tsp Himalayan salt

Instructions: 

Blend together all ingredients.
