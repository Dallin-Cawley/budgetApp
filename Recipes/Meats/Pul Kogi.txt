                          Pul Kogi (Korean Barbeque)
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) T. toasted mashed sesame seeds
(1/4) c. soy sauce
1 T. oil (sesame oil preferred)
3 T. sugar (maybe 4)
(1/4) c. chopped green onion
(1/2) t. garlic chopped fine or mashed
1/8 t. pepper
1 lb. meat
Instructions: Marinate at least one hour.
