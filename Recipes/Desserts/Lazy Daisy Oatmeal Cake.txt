                            Lazy Daisy Oatmeal Cake
Ingredients:
1 c. quick or regular oats
1 (1/4) c. boiling water
(1/2) c. butter softened
(3/4) c. sugar
(3/4) c. brown sugar; packed
1 t. vanilla
2 eggs
2 c. softened flour
(1/4) to (1/2) t. salt
1 t. soda
1 t. baking powder
1 t. cinnamon
(1/4) to (1/2) t. nutmeg
Instructions: Soak oats in boiling water for 20 minutes.  (put oats into boiling temperature water)  Beat butter until creamy; gradually add sugars and beat until fluffy.  Blend in vanilla; add eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition.  Add oat mixture, mix well.  Sift flour with salt, soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg and mix into oatmeal mixture.  Pour into prepared 9X13 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees and 30-40 minutes.  Do not remove from pan.  
Icing
(1/2) c. butter, melted
(1/2) to 2/3 c. finely packed brown sugar
(3/4) c. shredded coconut
(1/4). c. half and half cream or 3 T. canned milk
(1/2) c. chopped nuts
Melt all ingredients in saucepan.  Spread over warm cake.  Broil until bubbly and golden.
