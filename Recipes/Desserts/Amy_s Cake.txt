                                 Amy's Cake:
Ingredients:
1 cake mix
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1/8 t. salt
1 1/3 cups water
3 eggs
1 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
1 t. vanilla

Instructions:
Bake as directed on cake mix box.

Frosting:
(1/2) cup butter, softened
4 oz. cream cheese
Pinch of salt
Vanilla and/or almond extract
6 cups powdered sugar
Evaporated milk
