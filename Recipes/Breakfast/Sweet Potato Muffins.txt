                             Sweet Potato Muffins
Ingredients:
1 c. all purpose flour + 1 c. wheat flour
1 c. sugar
2 t. cinnamon
(1/2) t. salt
1 t. baking powder
1 egg
2 c. cold mashed sweet potatoes
(1/2) c. coconut oil
(1/2) c. applesauce
(1/2) c. unsweetened coconut
Instructions: In a small bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, and baking powder.  In another bowl, whisk the egg, sweet potatoes, coconut oil, and applesauce.  Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.  Fill greased muffin cups two-thirds full.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15-18 minutes.  
Glaze: 1 c. confectioner's sugar
2 T. plus 1-1/2 t. milk
1-1/2 t. butter
1 t. vanilla extract
(1/2) t. cinnamon
