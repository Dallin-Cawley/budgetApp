                          Chicken Spaghetti Casserole
                                       
2 c. spaghetti (cooked)
3 c. chicken cut up
1 c. minced pimento and peppers
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 c. chicken broth
(1/4) t. celery salt
(1/4) t. pepper
1 small onion cut up
(1/2) lb. diced cheddar cheese

Cook spaghetti in salted water until barely tender and drain well.  Combine with remaining ingredients and put in a baking dish.  Put crushed corn flakes mixed with a little mangarine on top.  Bake 1 hour at 350.
