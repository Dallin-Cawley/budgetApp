                               Mini Cheesecakes
Ingredients:
1 c. graham cracker crumbs
(3/4) c. plus 2 T. sugar, divided
3 T. butter, melted
3 pkg. cream cheese (8 oz. each), softened
1 t. vanilla
3 eggs
1 c. plus 2 T. coconut
54 speckled chocolate eggs
Instructions: Heat oven 325.  Mix graham cracker crumbs, 2 T. sugar, and butter.  Press into the bottoms of 18 paper lined muffin cups.  Beat cream cheese, vanilla, and remaining sugar with mixer until smooth.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing on low speed after each just until blended.  Spoon over crusts.  Bake 25-30 minutes or until centers and almost set.  Cool completely.  Refrigerate 2 hours.  Top each cheesecake with 1 T. coconut (toasted) shaped into a nest.  Fill each nest with 3 speckled chocolate eggs.
