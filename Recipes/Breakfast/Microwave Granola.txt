                       Microwave Granola (Yield: 6 cups)
Ingredients:
3 c. uncooked old fashioned oats
1/3 c. wheat germ
1/3 c. shredded coconut
1/3 c. sesame seeds
1/3 c. nuts
1/3 c. brown sugar
(1/2) t. cinnamon
1/3 c. vegetable oil, coconut oil, butter, or peanut butter
(1/4) c. honey
1 t. vanilla
(1/2) c. raisins
Instructions: 
   1. Place oats in ungreased, 2 quart utility dish.  Heat in microwave on full power for 2 (1/2) minutes.  2.  Add wheat germ, coconut, sesame seeds, nuts, brown sugar, and cinnamon to oats.  Stir to blend.  3.  Add oil, honey and vanilla to dry ingredients.  Toss lightly until coated.  Cook in microwave on Bake level for about 6 minutes, or until heated through.  Stir twice during cooking.  4.  Add raisins,  cook in microwave on full power for 1 to 2 minutes.
