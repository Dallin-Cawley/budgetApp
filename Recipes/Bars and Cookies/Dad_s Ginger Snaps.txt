                             Dad's Ginger Snaps
                                       
Ingredients:

1 (1/2) cups butter
2 cups sugar
(1/2) cup molasses
2 eggs
4 cups flour (2 white and 2 wheat for a hearty flavor)
2 tsp soda
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp ginger
2 tsp cloves

Instructions: 

Thoroughly cream shortening and sugar. Add molasses and well beaten eggs. Add sifted dry ingredients and beat until smooth. Roll into balls the size of a walnut and dip or roll in granulated sugar. Place 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350Â° for 8-10 minutes.
