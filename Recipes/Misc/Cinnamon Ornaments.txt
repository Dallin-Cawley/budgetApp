                              Cinnamon Ornaments
Ingredients:
4.12 oz. ground cinnamon (about 1 c.)
(3/4) c. applesauce
Instructions: Combine cinnamon with applesauce to form a stiff dough.  Add additional applesauce if necessary.  Roll out dough to (1/2) in. thickness.  Cut with cookie cutters.  Make hole for ribbon.  Carefully place on wire rack to dry.  Let air dry several days, turning over occasionally.  Make 12 ornaments.
