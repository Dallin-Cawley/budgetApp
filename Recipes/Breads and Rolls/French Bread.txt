French Bread

Ingredients:
4 T. yeast
4 cups warm water
.5 cups sugar
.5 cups oil
4 t. salt
5 eggs
Flour
Sesame seeds

Instructions:
Mix all ingredients, but seeds.  Add just enough flour to make a non-sticky dough.  Let rise 1 hour.  Divide into 12 equal sections.  Roll out like long snakes about 12 inches and braid by 3s making 4 loaves.  Bake on cookie sheets at 350 for 30 minutes.  (spray pan with pam)  Take off immediately.  Optional:  spread one beaten egg on loaves before baking and then sprinkle with sesame seeds, garlic powder, parmesan plus with zesty red pepper, or poppy seeds.
