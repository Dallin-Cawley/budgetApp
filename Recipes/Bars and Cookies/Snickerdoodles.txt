                                Snickerdoodles
                                       
Ingredients:

2 (3/4) cups sifted flour
(1/2) tsp salt
1 (1/2) cups sugar
2 tsp cream of tartar
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup butter, softened
2 eggs
4 tsp cinnamon
4 Tbsp sugar

Instructions:

Mix and sift flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt. Cream butter and add 1 (1/2) cup sugar gradually until fluffy; add beaten eggs. Add dry sifted ingredients. Chill in fridge. Roll small balls in remaining cinnamon and sugar mixture. Bake about 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet for about 10 minutes at 400Â°.
