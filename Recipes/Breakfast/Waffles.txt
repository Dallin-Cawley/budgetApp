                                    Waffles
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
2 t. baking powder
1 t. soda
(1/4) t. salt
2 eggs, separated
2 c. buttermilk
1/3 c. melted shortening or oil
Instructions: Sift together dry ingredients.  Separate the eggs and beat the whites until light and fluffy.  Set aside.  Beat the yolks, buttermilk, and oil.  Add to dry mixture.  Fold in the beaten egg whites.
