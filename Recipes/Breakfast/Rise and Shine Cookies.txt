                            Rise and Shine Cookies
Ingredients:
(1/2) c all purpose flour
(1/4) c. whole wheat flour
(1/2) t. baking soda
(1/4) t. salt
(1/4) t. ground cinnamon
1/8 t. ground nutmeg
(1/2) c. brown sugar
Egg substitute = 1 egg
3 T. vegetable oil
1 (1/4) c. quick cooking or regular oats
(1/2) c. wheat germ
Instructions: Preheat oven to 350.  Stir together flours, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Set aside.  Combine brown sugar, egg substitute, and vegetable oil.  Stir in flour mixture, oats, and wheat germ.  Drop by tablespoons about 1 inch apart on a baking sheet.  Flatten slightly.  Bake 10 minutes or until light brown.
