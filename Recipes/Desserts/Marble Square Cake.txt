                              Marble Square Cake
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1 egg
1/3 c. sugar
Vanilla
(1/2) c. margarine
(3/4) c. water
32 squares unsweetened chocolate
2 c. flour
2 eggs
1 t. soda
2 c. sugar
1 (1/2) c. sour cream (yogurt)
(1/4) t. salt
2 t. vanilla
Instructions: 
First bracket:  Mix and set aside.  
Second bracket: boil and melt.  
Third bracket: mix; add chocolate mixture to it.  Put in greased 12X17 cake pan.  Blot cream cheese mix on top.  Swirl.  Drop 1 c. chocolate chips on top.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
