
Danielle & Libby's Pumpkin Choc Chip Muffins
( makes 2 dozen)
Ingredients:
3 c. all- purpose flour
1 T. plus 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice (I like extra cinnamon & cloves)
2 t. baking soda
1 (1/2) t. salt
2 c. sugar
1 (15oz.) can pumpkin
4 lg. eggs
(1/2) c. vegetable oil
(1/2) c. milk or vanilla yogurt
(1/2) c. mini choc chips

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F.  Grease or line 30 muffin cups.  Combine flour, pumpkin spice, baking soda, and salt in large bowl.  Combine sugar, pumpkin, eggs, oil, and milk.  Mix until well blended.  Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture; stir just until moistened.  Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups, filling (3/4) full.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until done.
