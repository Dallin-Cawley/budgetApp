                             Southwestern Chicken
Ingredients:
2 cans (15 (1/4) oz. each) whole-kernel corn, drained
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 jar (16 oz.) chunky salsa, divided
6 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (4 oz. each)
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions: In a 5 qt. slow cooker, combine corn, black beans, and (1/2) c. salsa.  Top with chicken; pour remaining salsa over chicken.  Cover and cook on high 3-4 hours or on low 7-8 hours or until meat juices run clear.  Sprinkle with cheese; cover until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.  Makes 6 servings.
