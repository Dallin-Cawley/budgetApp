                             Raspberry Mint Crush
Ingredients:
(1/4) c. sugar
(1/2) c. lightly packed mint leaves
1 c. boiling water
1-10 oz. pkg. frozen raspberries
1-6 oz. lemonade
Instructions: Combine sugar, mint, and water.  Let set 5 minutes.  Add raspberries and concentrate.  Stir until thawed.  Strain into chilled pitcher half full of ice.  Add cold water (2 c.) Stir.
