                            Fusilli with Salsa Rosa
                                       
Ingredients:

8 oz. fusilli pasta
1 lb. ground beef or Italian sausage, casing removed and crumbled
3 garlic cloves, minced
(1/4) tsp dried red pepper flakes
1 cup sliced mushrooms
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
(1/2) cup heavy cream
(1/4) cup fresh chopped basil or parsley
Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions: 

Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Meanwhile, in large skillet over medium heat, saute ground beef, garlic, and dried red pepper flakes for 3-4 minutes or until brown; drain. Add mushrooms and crushed tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer for 10 minutes. Add heavy cream; simmer 2 more minutes. Add fusilli and basil; cook 1-2 minutes or until heated through. Serve with parmesan cheese. Makes 4 servings.
