                                Cranberry Salsa
Ingredients:
1 bag fresh cranberries
(1/2) bunch cilantro
1 bunch green onion (bottoms and half the greens)
(1/2) jalapeno (to taste) seeded
Lime juice (one lime)
(1/2) c. sugar
Instructions: Blend first four ingredients in processor.  Add lime juice and sugar.  Refrigerate 12-24 hours.
