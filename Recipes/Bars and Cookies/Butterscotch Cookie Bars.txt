                           Butterscotch Cookies Bars
                                       
Ingredients:

2 beaten eggs
1 cup sugar
(3/4) cup margarine
2 cups mini marshmallows
2 (1/2) cups crushed graham crackers
1 cup coconut
12 oz. package butterscotch chips
2 Tbsp peanut butter

Instructions: 

Combine the eggs, sugar, and margarine. Boil slowly for 2 minutes and cool. Add marshmallows, graham crackers, and coconut. Spray a 9x13 pan. Press ingredients into pan and let cool. Melt butterscotch chips and peanut butter together. Spread over the top of bars.
