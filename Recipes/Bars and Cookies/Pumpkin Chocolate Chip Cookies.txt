                        Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies
                                       
Ingredients:

(1/2) cup butter
(1/2) cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 (1/2) cups pumpkin
2 (1/2) cups flour
(3/4) tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions: 

Cream butter and sugar together. Stir in eggs, vanilla, and pumpkin. Add flour, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, and baking powder. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls onto greased baking sheet. Bake at 350Â° for 15 minutes.
