                             Cherry Orange Delight
Ingredients:
2 (1/2) c. boiling water
3 pkg. (4 serving size each) cherry flavored gelatin
2 (3/4) c. cold water
11 oz. can mandarin oranges, drained
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 T. sugar
8 oz. cool whip topping, thawed
Instructions: Stir boiling water into gelatin in medium bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.  Stir in cold water.  Refrigerate about 1 hour or until slightly thickened (consistency of unbeaten egg whites) Reserve a few mandarin oranges for garnish.  Gently stir remaining oranges into gelatin; set aside.  Beat cream cheese and sugar in separate bowl with wire whisk until well blended.  Gently stir in cool whip topping.  Spoon into large serving bowl, cover with gelatin mixture.  Refrigerate 2 hours or until firm.  Garnish with remaining oranges.
