                            Chocolate Zucchini Cake
Ingredients:
(1/2) t. cinnamon
(1/2) t. baking soda
2 c. grated zucchini
1 (1/2) c. flour
5 T. cocoa 
(1/2) c. buttermilk (or (1/2) c. milk + 1 T. vingar)
(1/2) c. margarine
1 (3/4) c. sugar
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
(1/2) t. salt
Instructions: Combine all ingredients and pour into a 9X13-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with chocolate chips and chopped walnuts if desired.  Bakes at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
