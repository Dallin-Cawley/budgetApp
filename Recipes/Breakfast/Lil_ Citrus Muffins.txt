                             Lil' Citrus Muffins
Ingredients:
1 c. all purpose flour
(1/2) t. baking powder
(1/4) t. baking soda
1/8 t. salt
(1/4) c. butter or margarine, softened
2/3 c. sugar
1 egg
(1/2) sour cream
(1/2) t. vanilla
2 t. orange, lemon, and lime zest
Topping:
2 T. butter or margarine, melted
1/3 c. sugar
Instructions: Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In small bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.  Mix well.  Beat (1/4) c. softened butter and 2/3 c. sugar until well blended.  Add egg; beat until smooth.  Mix in sour cream, vanilla, and orange zest.  Add flour mixture; mix until dry ingredients are moistened.  Bake 12-13 minutes.  For topping, dip tops of warm muffins in butter and then into sugar.  Serve warm.
