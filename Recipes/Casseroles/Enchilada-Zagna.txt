                                Enchilada-Zagna
Ingredients:
2-3 cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into sm. pieces (about 3 cups)
28 oz. can or 2-15 oz. cans mild red enchilada sauce
8 oz. sour cream
12 oz. can evaporated milk
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz. can diced green chilies, undrained
12 corn tortillas (7 inch)
Instructions: Preheat oven to 350.  Grease a 9X13 pan.  Combine enchilada sauce and sour cream in medium bowl.  Combine milk, cheese, and chilies in medium saucepan.  Cook over medium heat until cheese has melted and mixture is smooth.  Remove from heat.  Spread 1 cup enchilada sauce mixture on bottom of prepared dish.  Layer with 4 tortillas, 1 cup enchilada sauce mixture, 1 (1/2) cups chicken, 1 cup cheese sauce.  Repeat layers one more time, started with tortillas.  Top with remaining tortillas, enchilada sauce mixture, and cheese sauce.  Cover with foil.  Bake for 40 minutes.  Uncover; cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.
