                               Cranberry Relish
                                       
12 oz. fresh cranberries
1 med. orange
1 c. sugar

Cut orange into 8 pieces.  Keep peel on and get rid of seeds.  Put (1/2) of the orange and cranberries into a food processor.  Grind it up.  Dump in bowl.  Repeat with second half.  Dump into bowl.  Stir in sugar.
