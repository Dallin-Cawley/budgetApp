                             Butterscotch Brownies
                                       
Ingredients:

2 cups sifted flour
1 (1/2) tsp salt
(1/2) cup butter
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla 
2 tsp baking powder
2 cups butterscotch chips (12 oz. bag)
2 cups packed brown sugar

Instructions: 

Combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside. Combine butterscotch pieces and butter. Melt in a bowl over hot water. Remove from heat and place in a big bowl. Stir in brown sugar and cool for 5 minutes. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Blend in flour mixture. Spread in greased pan and bake at 350Â° for 30 minutes. Cool, cut, and enjoy!
