                       Cherries Jubilee Cheesecake Bars
                                       
Ingredients:

1 box cherry chip cake mix
(1/2) cup butter or margarine, softened
2-8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 container cherry frosting (or make your own)
3 eggs

Instructions: 

Heat oven to 325Â°. In large bowl, beat cake mix and butter on low speed until crumbly; reserve 1 cup. Press remaining crumbly mixture in bottom of ungreased 9x13 pan. In the same bowl, beat cream cheese and frosting on medium speed until smooth. Beat in eggs until blended. Pour over crust; sprinkle with reserved crumbly mixture. Bake about 45 minutes or until set. Cool completely. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours until chilled.
