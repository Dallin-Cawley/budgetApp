                               Biscuits Supreme
Ingredients:
2 c. sifted all purpose flour
4 t. baking powder
(1/2) t. salt
(1/2) t. cream of tartar
2 t. sugar
(1/2) c. shortening
2/3 c. milk
Instructions: Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cream of tartar, and sugar; cut in shortening till mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Add milk all at once; stir only till dough follows fork around bowl.  Turn out on lightly floured surface; knead gently (1/2) a minute.  Pat or roll (1/2) an inch thick.  Cut with cutter.  Bake on ungreased pan 10-12 minutes at 450 degrees.
