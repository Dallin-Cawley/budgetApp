                                 Refried Beans
                                       
Ingredients:

1 lb. beans
(1/2) cup olive oil
small onion, diced
garlic
2 tsp salt
(1/4)-(1/2) tsp oregano
2-3 tsp cumin
4 tsp chili powder
5 dozen 10 inch tortillas

Instructions:

Let cool in pan. Blend (make sure the water is even with beans). Warm 1 dozen tortilla in package for 2 minutes.
