                              Julie's Playdough
Ingredients:
2 c. water
2 c. flour
(1/2) c. salt
4 t. cream of tartar or 2 T. alum
2 T. salad oil food coloring
Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a pot.  Cook, stirring constantly until mixture congeals-it will look terrible.  Plop onto a breadboard and knead.  May add color when cooking or kneading.
