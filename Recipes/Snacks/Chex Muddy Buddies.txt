                              Chex Muddy Buddies
                                       
1 c. semisweet chocolate chips
(1/2) c. peanut butter
(1/4) c. butter or margarine
1 t. vanilla
9 c. Chex cereal (any variety)
1 (1/2) c. powdered sugar

Melt chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter in microwave until smooth.  Stir in vanilla.  Pour chocolate mixture over cereal stirring until evenly coated.  Place cereal mixture into 2 gallon food storage bag.  Add the powdered sugar to the bag and shake until evenly coated.  Spread on waxed paper to cool.
