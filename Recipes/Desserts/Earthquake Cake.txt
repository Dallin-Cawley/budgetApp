                                Earthquake Cake
Ingredients:
1 c. pecans
1 box German chocolate cake or devils food
1 stick oleo
4 c. powdered sugar
1 c. coconut
8 oz. cream cheese
1 t. vanilla
(1/2) c. chocolate or white chocolate chips
Instructions:  Put nuts and coconut in a 9X13 pan.  Prepare cake mix as directed.  Pour on coconut and nuts.  Beat together oleo, powdered sugar, cream cheese, and vanilla.  Spoon on cake mix in blobs.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.
