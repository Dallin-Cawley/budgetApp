                                  Sesame Beef
Ingredients:
1 (1/2) lbs. boneless beef chuck roast, sliced into thin strips
1 1/3 c. beef broth
(1/2) c. oyster sauce
(1/2)-1 lb. fresh broccoli florets
(1/4) c. water
2 T. cornstarch
Hot, cooked rice, optional
Toasted sesame seeds (for garnish)
Instructions: In a slow-cooker, arrange beef strips; pour beef broth and oyster sauce over the beef.  Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours or until beef is very tender.  Place broccoli in microwave safe bowl with water.  Microwave 4-5 minutes until broccoli is tender.  In a cup, combine cornstarch and 2 T. cooking liquid; stir until smooth.  Add to slow-cooker and stir well to combine.  Drain broccoli and stir into the slow cooker.  Cook uncovered on high for about 15 minutes or until sauce is thickened.  Serve over hot, cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds.
