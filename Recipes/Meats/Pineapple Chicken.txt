                               Pineapple Chicken
Ingredients:
Chicken breasts
Egg
Cornstarch
Pineapple rings
Sauce: Combine
(3/4) c. sugar
(1/4) c. red wine vinegar
3 T. ketchup
(1/4) c. green onions
1 T. soy sauce (light)
(1/2) t. salt
(1/4) c. pineapple juice
Instructions: Brown chicken breasts in hot oiled skillet after dipping in egg and cornstarch.  Place a pineapple ring on each breast in a 9X13 pan.  Pour sauce over breasts and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
