                             Lemon Zucchini Bread
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter, softened
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 (1/2) t. grated lemon zest
1 T. fresh lemon juice
2 c. flour
1 t. baking powder
(1/2) t. baking soda
(1/2) t. salt
2 c. grated zucchini
(1/2) c. nuts (optional)
Instructions: Cream butter with sugar until smooth.  Add the eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice; beat until light.  Stir flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together; add to creamed mixture.  Stir in zucchini and nuts.  Divide batter evenly into 2 greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.  To prevent burning, move bread to a higher rack or lower temp until bread in completely cooked.
