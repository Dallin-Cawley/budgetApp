                               Christmas Treats
Ingredients:
1 box golden grahams (12 c.)
1 box rice chex (12 c.)
6 oz. shredded coconut
8 oz. sliced almonds (toasted)
2 c. white karo syrup
1 (1/2) c. sugar
1 (1/2) cubes margarine
(1/2) t. almond extract
Instructions: Lightly toss the cereals, coconut, and toasted almonds together.  Boil the karo, sugar, margarine, and extract for 5 mintues.  Pour over cereal and nut mixture and toss.  Store in a covered container.
