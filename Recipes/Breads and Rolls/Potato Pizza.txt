Potato Pizza

Ingredients:
Dough:
1.25 cups warm water
1 t. yeast
1 t. sugar
1 t. salt
3.25 cup flour
5 med. potatoes

Instructions:
Dissolve yeast in water; add remaining ingredients; knead for a few minutes until dough is pliable, but not sticky; let rise 30 minutes-1 (1/2) hours.  Punch down dough and roll out to fil a 10X13 greased pan.  Let rise for another 30 minutes to 1 hour.  Cook potatoes briefly (2 min) in microwave; just prior to assembling pizza, peel and grate potatoes; spread grated potatoes on pizza, then drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and fresh rosemary if desired.  Bake in preheated oven (450) for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden.  Sprinkle with grated mozzarella.
