Honey Party Buns

Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, cut into pieces
.5 cup honey
2 cup boiling water
2 pkg. active dry yeast (1/4 oz. each)
.33 cup warm water
2 eggs, beaten
1 t. baking powder
2 t. salt
3.5 cup whole wheat flour
4.5 cup all-purpose flour
Sliced ham, cheese, and lettuce, optional

Instructions:
Place butter and honey in large mixing bowl; pour the boiling water over and set aside to cool.  In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water; set aside.  Add eggs, baking powder, and salt to honey mixture.  Add yeast mixture and whole wheat flour; beat until smooth.  Stir in enough flour to form a soft dough.  Knead for 8-10 minutes.  Place in greased bowl and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.  Punch down dough and divide in half.  Roll each half to (1/2) in. thickness; cut out buns.  Place on greased baking sheets.  Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.  Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes.  Makes 4-5 dozen.
