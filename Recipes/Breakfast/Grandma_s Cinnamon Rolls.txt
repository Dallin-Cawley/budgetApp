                          Grandma's Cinnamon Rolls
Ingredients:
2 c. warm milk
(1/2) c. sugar
(1/2) c. butter
2 T. yeast, dissolved in (1/2) c. warm water, and 1 t. sugar
3 eggs
2 t. salt
6-8 c. flour
Instructions: Dissolve yeast and set aside.  Beat eggs; add butter, sugar, and salt.  Add half the flour and yeast mixture.  Add remaining flour, alternating with warm milk.  Let rise till double in size.  Roll out into cinnamon rolls.  Place on greased sheets and let rise till doubled again.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15-18 minutes.
