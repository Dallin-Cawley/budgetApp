                         Pepperoni Pizza Snowman Pasta
                                       
Ingredients:

14 oz. bag pasta
26 oz. jar prepared pasta sauce
4 oz. pepperoni, cut into halves (regular, turkey, or reduced fat)
1 tsp Italian seasoning
2 cups mozzarella cheese, grated

Instructions: 

Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and place in large saucepan along with remaining ingredients. Heat and stir until hot throughout. Season to taste and enjoy! Serves 6-8.
