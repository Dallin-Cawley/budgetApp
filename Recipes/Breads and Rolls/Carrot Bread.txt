Carrot Bread
                                       
Ingredients:

2 cups sugar
3 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
3 tsp cinnamon
1 cup oil
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup crushed pineapple (with juice)
2 cups grated carrots (packed)
3 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions: 

Mix together sugar, flour, soda, salt, and cinnamon. Add oil, eggs, pineapple, carrots, vanilla, and walnuts. Grease and flour 2 large loaf pans, and bundt pan, or a 9x13. Bake at 325ยบ for 60-75 minutes.
