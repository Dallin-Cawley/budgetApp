                          Perfect Party Mini Quiches
Ingredients:
1 can evaporated milk
3 eggs, beaten
2 T. all-purpose flour
(1/4) t. salt
(1/4) t. ground black pepper
2 c. shredded mild or sharp cheddar cheese
2 c. chopped, frozen broccoli, thawed and drained
(1/2) c. chopped red bell pepper
Instructions: Heat oven to 350.  Grease and lightly flour 12 2 (1/2) inch muffin cups.  Whisk evaporated milk, eggs, flour, salt, and black pepper in medium bowl until blended.  Stir in cheese, broccoli, and bell pepper.  Spoon (1/4)-1/3 c. of mixture into each prepared muffin cup, filling almost to rim.  Stir mixture frequently to evenly distribute ingredients.  Bake for 23-28 minutes or until knife comes out clean and tops are lightly browned.  Cool in pans for 15 minutes.
