                                Caramel Popcorn
                                       
8-10 qts. Popcorn
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. sugar
2 sticks butter
(1/2) c. light karo syrup
(1/2) t. soda
1/8 t. cream of tartar
dash of salt

Bring sugar, butter, and syrup to boil.  Add rest of ingredients.  Pour over popcorn then bake at 200 for one hour.
