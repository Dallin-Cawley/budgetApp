                            Carrot and Corn Chowder
Ingredients:
1 t. oil
3 medium carrots, thinly sliced (about 1 c.)
10 (3/4) oz. can condensed cream of celery
11 oz. can Mexican style corn
3 green onions, thinly sliced
(1/4) t. caraway seeds
1 (1/2) soup cans milk
1 heaping c. shredded cabbage
Instructions: In a medium pan, cook carrots, onions, and caraway seeds in oil until carrots are slightly soft.  Stir in soup, milk, corn, and cabbage.  Heat to boiling over medium heat.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15 minute or until carrots and cabbage are tender.
