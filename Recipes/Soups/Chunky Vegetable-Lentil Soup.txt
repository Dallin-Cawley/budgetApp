                         Chunky Vegetable-Lentil Soup
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 med. Onion, cut into thin rings
1 clove garlic, minced
1 c. dry green lentils, rinsed and drained
1 lb. whole sm. mushrooms (halve or quarter large ones)
4 med. Carrots, thinly sliced (2 cups)
2 celery stalks, chopped
4 c. water
14 oz. can vegetable broth
(1/4) t. salt
(1/4) t. ground black pepper
(1/4) of a head napa or red cabbage, sliced into strips (2 cups)
Instructions: In a 4 quart saucepan or dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic; cook for 4-5 minutes or until onion is tender, stirring occasionally.  Stir in lentils; cook and stir 1 minute.  Add mushrooms, carrots, celery, water, vegetable broth, salt, and pepper.  Bring to boiling.  Reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 25 minutes or until lentils are tender.  Divide among soups bowls; top with cabbage.
