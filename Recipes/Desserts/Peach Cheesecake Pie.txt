                             Peach Cheesecake Pie
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
3 T. cornstarch
2 c. cold water
3 oz. pkg. jello (peach)-(can double the jello)
 
Instructions: Mix together and bring to boil.  Boil 2 minutes.  Cool thoroughly, and then add sliced fruit.  

Filling:
1 c. whipped cream
2 T. sugar
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
2 t. vanilla
Beat all together and pour into prepared graham cracker crusts.
