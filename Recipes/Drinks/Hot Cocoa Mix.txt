                                 Hot Cocoa Mix
Ingredients:
2 c. nonfat dry milk powder
(3/4) c. sugar
(1/2) c. unsweetened cocoa
(1/2) c. mini semisweet chocolate chips
(1/2) c. powdered nondairy creamer
1/8 t. salt
Instructions: Whisk all ingredients together until they are evenly blended.  Makes about 4 cups of mix.
