                           Soft Gingerbread Cookies
                                       
Ingredients:

1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup shortening
1 cup molasses
1 Tbsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp ginger
2 tsp cloves
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
4 (1/2) cups flour

Instructions: 

Cream sugar, eggs, and shortening in bowl. Add molasses and mix well. Add everything else in order and mix. Roll out and cut with cookie cutters. Bake at 350Â° for 9 minutes. Frost and store in airtight container.
