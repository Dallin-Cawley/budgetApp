--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cheesy Mexican Rice Casserole 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6 cups cooked brown rice (I used the quick "Boil in a Bag" kind to speed things up) 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(1/2) cup fat free refried beans 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 can rotel tomatoes (original) 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2 Tbsp tomato paste 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2 cups shredded Mexican cheese, plus a little more for topping
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2 Tbsp Mexican seasoning
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mexican seasoning: 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(makes 3 (1/2) Tbsp) 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 Tbsp chili powder 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 Tbsp cumin 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 (1/2) tsp paprika 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 (1/2) tsp garlic powder 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 (1/2) tsp onion powder 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preheat oven to 375Â°. Cook your brown rice; set aside in a large bowl. Into the brown rice, add the tomato paste, Mexican seasoning, rotel, cheese, and refried beans. Stir everything together until completely combined. Season to taste with salt. You will need a pretty decent amount because none is added before this point. Spread into an oval baking dish (9x13 baking dish will also work). Top with more Mexican cheese. Bake for 15 minutes. (optional) Top with chopped cilantro.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Makes 10 servings
