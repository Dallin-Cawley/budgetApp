                              Vegetable Bean Soup
Ingredients:
3 cans great northern bean, drained and rinsed
4 c. water
2 cans hominy or 2 cans garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1-16 oz. pkg. frozen shoepeg corn
1-15 oz. crushed or diced tomatoes, undrained
1-11 oz. can condensed bean and bacon coup, undiluted
1-10 oz. can diced tomatoes and green chilies, undrained
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 T. dried cilantro flakes
1 T. dried parsley flakes
1 t. ground cumin
2 bay leaves
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions: In dutch oven, combine the first 13 ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes.  Discard bay leaves.  Sprinkle with cheese.
