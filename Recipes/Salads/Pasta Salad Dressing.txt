                             Pasta Salad Dressing
Ingredients:
(1/2)-3/4 c. sugar
2/3 c. olive oil + (1/2) c. water
1 1/4 c. white vinegar 
(1/2) t. celery seed
1 t. onion salt
(1 t. salt, if needed)
(3/4) t. pepper
1 t. seasoning salt
2 t. dry mustard
2 t. garlic powder
1 t. parsley
Instructions: Mix all together well.  Can marinate vegetables in dressing prior to adding to pasta.
