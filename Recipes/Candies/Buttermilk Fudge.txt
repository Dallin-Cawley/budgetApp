                               Buttermilk Fudge
Ingredients:
2 c. granulated sugar
(3/4) c. butter
1 c. buttermilk
1 t. baking soda
2 T. light corn syrup
2 T. vanilla extract
1 c. chopped walnuts
Instructions: In heavy saucepan, combine sugar, butter, buttermilk, baking soda, and corn syrup over medium-high heat.  Cook, stirring occasionally until mixture reaches hard ball stage (268 degrees on candy thermometer)  Remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla and pecans.  Place pan in a large bowl of ice and beat with a wooden spoon just until mixture begins to thicken.  Quickly spread in a greased 8 inch square pan.  Allow to cool thoroughly before cutting into 1 inch squares.
