                              Buttermilk Pancakes
Ingredients:
2 eggs, unbeaten
2 c. buttermilk 
(1/4) c. butter, melted
2 c. flour
1 (1/4) t. baking soda
(3/4) t. baking powder
(1/2) t. salt
(1/4)-1/2 chocolate or cinnamon chips
Instructions: Mix together wet ingredients.  Combine dry ingredients with chips, then stir into wet ingredients, just until combined.  Cook on hot griddle (325).
