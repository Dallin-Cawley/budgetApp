                     Pasta with Tomatoes, Feta, and Herbs
                                       
Ingredients:

14 oz. bag of pasta
2 cloves garlic, minced
6 Tbsp olive oil
4 ripe tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
2 tsp dried Italian seasoning or (1/2) cup chopped fresh herbs
1 tsp salt or to taste
(1/2) tsp ground black pepper

Instructions: 

Add all ingredients to hot pasta and carefully blend to combine. Correct seasoning. Serve warm.
