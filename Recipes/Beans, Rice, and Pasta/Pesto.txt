                                     Pesto
                                       
Ingredients:

2 cups fresh basil leaves (wash then stuff in measuring cup)
2 Tbsp pine nuts
1 tsp salt, to taste
1-2 beams garlic
1 Tbsp parmesan cheese
(1/4) cup olive oil

Instructions: 

Blend in a food processor or blender to make puree. Put on cooked pasta. Sprinkle with more pine nuts and parmesan cheese.
