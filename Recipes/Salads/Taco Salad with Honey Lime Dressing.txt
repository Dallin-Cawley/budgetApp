                      Taco Salad with Honey Lime Dressing
Ingredients:
Honey lime dressing: 
(1/4) c. honey
Juice of 3 limes
2 T. white vinegar
1 T. cumin
(1/2) t. kosher salt
(1/4) t. fresh ground pepper
1/3 c. olive oil
Salad:
1 head of Romaine, shredded
3 green onions, sliced
1 red pepper, diced
Half red onion, sliced thin
2 ear grilled corn (or bag of thawed frozen corn)
Half bunch of cilantro
Tomatoes
Grilled chicken(seasoned with salt and pepper, and a little cumin) also good marinated
(1/2) c. shredded cheese
Tortilla chips, crushed
Favorite type of beans
Instructions: Dressing: Put everything except the olive oil in a bowl and whisk until combined.  While whisking, slowly pour in the olive oil.  Whisk until smooth.  Add more of any ingredients that you feel it needs.  Salad: Put everything except for the chips and beans in a large bowl.  Add a little dressing.  Add the chips and beans on top.  Garnish with sliced limes.  Add more dressing if you feel it needs it.
