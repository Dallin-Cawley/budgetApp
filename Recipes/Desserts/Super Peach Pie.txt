                                Super Peach Pie
Ingredients:
5 large peaches
Cooked pie shell
1 c. sugar
3 T. corn starch
(1/2) c. water
(1/2) t. almond flavoring
2 T. butter
Sliced peaches
 
Instructions: Slice three large peaches into a cooked pie shell.  Mash two more peaches and add the sugar, corn starch, and water.  Cook 5 minutes until it boils, then add almond flavoring and butter.  Pour over slices peaches.  Chill.  Serve with whipped cream.
