                                Navy Bean Soup
Ingredients:
5 (1/2) c. chicken broth or bouillon
1 c. dried small white beans, sorted and rinsed
1 (1/2) c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped onion
2 carrots, chopped
2 oz. turkey ham, finely chopped
(3/4) t. dried thyme
(1/4) t. ground black pepper
1 bay leaf
Instructions: In a large saucepan, combine the broth or bouillon and beans.  Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat.  Cover and simmer for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until the beans are tender.  Add the celery, onion, carrots, turkey ham, thyme, pepper, and bay leaf.  Cover and simmer about 30 minutes or until the veggies are tender.  Remove and discard the bay leaf before serving.
