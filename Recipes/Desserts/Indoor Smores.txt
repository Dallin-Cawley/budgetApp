                               Indoor S'mores
Ingredients:
8 c. honey graham cereal
5 c. marshmallows
1 (1/2) c. milk chocolate chips
(1/4) c. light corn syrup
5 T. butter or margarine
Instructions: Butter 9X13.  In a large bowl, toss cereal with 1 c. marshmallows.  Melt marshmallows, chocolate chips, butter, and karo until smooth.  Pour over cereal and stir.  Press into 9X13 and cut into squares.
