Bread Sticks
                                       
Ingredients:

1 Tbsp malted milk
1 Tbsp honey
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp yeast
1 .5cups warm water
3.5 cups flour

Instructions:

Dissolve yeast in warm water and honey. Add malted milk, salt and flour. Knead. Let rise 20 minutes. Cut in 8. Roll out each unit and cut into strips. Place on cookie sheet and let rise again. Bake at 400ยบ for 15 minutes. Brush tops with melted butter and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
