                              Cinnamon Swirl Loaf
Ingredients:
1 pkg. active dry yeast (1 T.)
(1/4) c. water
2 c. milk, scalded
(1/2) c. sugar
(1/2) c. shortening
2 t. salt
7 (1/2) -8 c. sifted all purpose flour
2 eggs
(3/4) c. sugar
1 (1/2) T. cinnamon
Soft butter
Instructions: Soften yeast in warm water.  Pour scaled milk over (1/2) c. sugar.  Cool to lukewarm.  Add 3 c. flour, mix well.  Stir in softened yeast and eggs, salt, and shortening.  Beat well.  Add enough of remaining flour to make a soft dough.  Turn out on lightly floured surface.  Cover and let rest 10 minutes.  Knead till smooth and elastic (8-10 min) Place in lightly greased bowl, turning once to grease surface.  Cover and let rise in warm place till dough in size (60-90 min)  Punch down and let rise again till almost double (60 min)  Punch down and divide dough in half.  Cover and let rest 10 minutes.  Roll each half in 15 X 7 inch rectangle, and (1/2) inch thick.  Mix (3/4) c. sugar and cinnamon.  Reserve 2 T. sugar mixture, sprinkle remainder over rectangles of dough.  Sprinkle about 2 t. of water over each; smooth with spatula.  Roll each as if for jelly roll, beginning with narrow side.  Seal long edge.  Place sealed edge down in 2 greased 9 (1/2) X 2 X3 loaf pans.  Let rise till almost double (45-60 min) Just before baking, brush loaves with soft butter and sprinkle with remaining cinnamon sugar.  Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes or till done.  Makes two loaves.
