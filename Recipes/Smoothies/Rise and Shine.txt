                                Rise and Shine
                                       
Ingredients:

(1/2) banana
2 cups strawberries
(1/2) cup mango
1 Tbsp honey
1 cup almond milk
(1/2) cup oats

Instructions:

Step 1.
Cut and measure out ingredients.

Step 2.
Place ingredients in labeled plastic bags and freeze.

Step 3.
Blend 1 cup of liquid with ingredient pack.
