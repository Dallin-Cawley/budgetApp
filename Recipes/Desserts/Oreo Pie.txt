                                   Oreo Pie
Ingredients:
2 Oreo Pie crusts
12 oz. cream cheese
16 oz. cool whip
(3/4) c. sugar
1 lb. package Oreo cookies
 
Instructions: Mix together cream cheese and sugar.  Fold in cool whip.  Crumble Oreos and fold into mixture (1/2 -3/4 pkg.)  Put into shells and refrigerate or freeze, removing prior to serving.
