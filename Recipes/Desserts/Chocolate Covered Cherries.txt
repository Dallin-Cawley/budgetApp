                          Chocolate Covered Cherries
Ingredients:
1 box powdered sugar
4 T. butter
Pinch of salt
1 t. vanilla
4 T. sweetened condensed milk 
(1/4) t. almond extract
Cherries, nuts, caramels, and chocolate
Instructions: Cream butter and sugar together, adding milk in small amounts.  Add salt, vanilla, and almond extract.  Drain cherries.  Roll cherries in cream mixture to cover each cherry well.  Add nuts or coconut to left over fondant and shape as desired.  Melt chocolate over boiling water.  Dip cherries and other things one at a time in melted chocolate.  Lay on wax paper to cool.
