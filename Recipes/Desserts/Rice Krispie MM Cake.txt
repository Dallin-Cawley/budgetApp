                           Rice Krispie/M&M Cake
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, melted in large soup kettle, save butter wrapper to grease bundt pan and to help pat the finished product into the pan
2-10 oz. bags small white marshmallows, melt
14.5 oz. box of Rice Krispies
16 oz. bag of m&ms
Instructions: Pour cereal into melted marshmallows, stir.  When mixed, pour in m&ms.  Pat and push mixture into bundt pan.  Put the bundt pan over a low flame.  When the pan is heated through you will be able to turn the pan over and the cake will release.
