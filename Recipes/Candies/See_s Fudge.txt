                                 See's Fudge
Ingredients:
1 pkg. chocolate chips
(1/2) c. margarine
1 t. vanilla
2 c. sugar
(3/4) c. evaporated milk
12 lg. marshmallows
Instructions: Stir chips, margarine, and vanilla in lg. bowl.  Mix remaining ingredients in heavy pan and bring to boil.  Boil for 9 minutes over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Pour over ingredients in bowl and stir.  Pour into butter 8X8 pan and refrigerate.
