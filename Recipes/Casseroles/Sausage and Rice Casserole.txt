                          Sausage and Rice Casserole

4 (1/2) c. water
1 (1/2) pkg. chicken noodle soup (lipton paper pkg.)
1 lb. sausage
(1/2) bunch of celery
mushrooms (can)
small onion
green pepper
1 scant c. instant rice

Boil water.  Add noodle soup, add cut up celery, boil 10 minutes and add instant rice.  Boil slowly.  Place cut up sausage in fry pan, cook slowly, and take off grease.  Do not brown!  Add onion and cut up peppers.  Cook until both are sort of transparent.  Add to other mixture and simmer until done.  Add mushrooms a few minutes before serving.
