                                Snickers Fudge
Ingredients:
1 c. milk chocolate chips
(1/4) c. butterscotch chips
(1/4) c. peanut butter
4 T. butter
1 c. sugar
(1/4) c. evaporated milk
1 (1/2) c. marshmallow crème
(1/4) c. peanut butter
1 t. vanilla
1 (1/2) c. chopped salted peanuts
14 oz. pkg. caramel cubes
(1/4) c. heavy cream
1 c. milk chocolate chips
(1/4) c. butterscotch chips
(1/4) c. creamy peanut butter


Instructions: First Layer Prep: lightly spray 9X13 baking pan with cooking spray.  Over medium heat, melt milk chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butterscotch chips.  Stir together until completely melted and smooth, then pour into fudge pan.  Spread mixture evenly with spatula then refrigerate until set.  Second layer: Melt butter over medium-high heat.  Add sugar and evaporated milk and bring to boil.  Cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from the heat and quickly mix in marshmallow crème, peanut butter, and vanilla.  Add peanuts to mixture and spread evenly over chocolate layer.  Refrigerate until set.  Third layer: Melt caramels and heavy cream over low heat until smooth.  Pour over peanut mixture and refrigerate until set.  Fourth layer: Repeat first layer directions.  Pour over caramel mixture and refrigerate for at least one hour before cutting.
