                              Cinnamon Surprises
Ingredients:
1 pkg. buttermilk buscuits (can of 10)
10 large marshmellows
3 T. melted butter
3 T. sugar and 1 (1/2) t. cinnamon, mixed
Instructions:  Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Flatten out each biscuit to about a 2 inch circle.  Place a marshmallow in the center of the biscuit and wrap and biscuit around it.  Make sure it is completely covered and pinch the top.  Dip the whole thing in melted margarine then roll in cinnamon/sugar mixture.  Bake for 8-10 minutes in a greased muffin tin.
