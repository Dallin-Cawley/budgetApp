                            Moist Chocolate Muffins
                         (adapted from Allrecipes.com)
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
(3/4) c. sugar
(1/2) c. cocoa
(3/4) c. chocolate chips
1 t. baking soda
(1/4) t. salt
1 egg
1 c. plain yogurt
(1/2) c. milk
(1/4) c. vegetable oil 
(1/4) c. chocolate chips

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°.  Grease or line 12-16 muffin cups.
Combine flour, sugar, (3/4) c. chocolate chips, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.  Whisk egg, yogurt, milk, vanilla, and oil in another bowl until smooth.  Pour into chocolate mixture and stir until batter is just blended.  Fill prepared muffin cups (3/4) full and sprinkle with remaining (1/4) c. chocolate chips.  Bake for about 20 minutes.  Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks.
