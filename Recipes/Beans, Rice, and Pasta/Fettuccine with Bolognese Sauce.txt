                        Fettuccine with Bolognese Sauce
                                       
Ingredients:

1 Tbsp olive oil
1 large yellow onion, diced
3 celery stalks, diced
1 carrot, diced
4 garlic cloves, minced
(1/4) lb. pancetta, chopped
1 (1/2) lb. lean ground beef
1 cups dry white wine
1 cup whole milk
6 oz. can tomato paste
14 (1/2) oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
(1/4) tsp red pepper
2 Tbsp oregano, chopped fresh 
(1/3) cup fresh flat leaf parsley, chopped
2 (1/2) tsp kosher salt
(1/4) tsp black pepper
(1/8) tsp ground nutmeg
(1/4) cup grated parmesan

Instructions: 

In dutch oven, over medium heat, heat the oil. Add the onion and saute for 2 minutes. Add celery, carrots, and garlic and cook for 5 minutes more. Add the pancetta and cook for 5 minutes, then add the beef and cook until brown. Add the wine and the remaining ingredients and simmer for 45 minutes. Serve with cooked fettuccine. Sprinkle with parmesan.
