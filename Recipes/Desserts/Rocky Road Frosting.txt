                              Rocky Road Frosting
Ingredients:
2-1 oz. squares unsweetened chocolate
2 c. miniature marshmallows
(1/4) c. water
4 T. butter or margarine
2 (1/2) c. sifted confectioner's sugar
1 t. vanilla
(1/4) c. broken walnuts (optional)
Instructions:  In a 2 qt. heavy saucepan, combine chocolate, half the marshmallows, water and butter.  Heat until blended, stirring constantly.  Cool slightly; add sugar and vanilla; beat until thick.  Stir in remaining marshmallows.  Add nuts if desired.  Frosts top of one 13X9X2 inch cake.
