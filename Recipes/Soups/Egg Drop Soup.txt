                                 Egg Drop Soup
Ingredients:
4 c. chicken broth
1 T. cornstarch
1 well beaten egg
2 T. sliced green onion
Instructions: In saucepan slowly stir chicken broth into cornstarch.  Cook, stirring constantly until thickened.  Slowly pour in the well beaten egg; stir once gently.  Remove from heat.  Garnish with green onion.  Makes 4 servings.  Optional: add veggies or seasonings such as soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame seed oil, before adding the beaten egg.
