                                   Spudnuts
Ingredients:
(1/2) c. pototo water
3 eggs
1 (1/2) c. milk (don't scald-warm)
(1/2) c. sugar
1 c. cooked mashed potatoes
2 t. lemon extract
1 yeast cake
7 c. flour
nutmeg
Instructions: Moisten yeast in potato water.  Heat milk to warm and add other ingredients knead like bread dough.  Let rise 2 hours.  Roll out and cut with doughnut cutter.  Place on greased tray.  Let rise half an hour.  Fry in deep oil.  While hot, glaze and drain.  
Glaze: 1 (1/2) lbs powdered sugar, milk, and margarine.
