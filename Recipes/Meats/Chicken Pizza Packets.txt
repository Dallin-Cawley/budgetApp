                             Chicken Pizza Packets
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 T. olive of vegetable oil
1 sm. zucchini, thinly sliced
16 pepperoni slices
1 sm. green pepper, julienned
1 sm. onion, sliced
(1/2) t. dried oregano
(1/2) t. dried basil
(1/4) t. salt
(1/4) t. garlic powder
(1/4) t. pepper
1 c. halved cherry tomatoes
(1/2) c. shredded mozzarella cheese
(1/2) c. shredded parmesan cheese
Instructions: In large bowl, combine the first 11 ingredients.  Coat four pieces of heavy duty foil with nonstick cooking spray.  Place a quarter of the chicken mixture in the center of each piece.  Fold foil around mixture and seal tightly, grill, uncovered, over medium hot heat for 15-18 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.  Carefully open each packet.  Sprinkle with tomatoes and cheeses.  Seal loosely, grill 2 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.  Makes 4 servings.
