                                Cilantro Pesto
                                       
Ingredients:

1 (1/2) cups fresh cilantro leaves and tender stems (2 bunches)
2 garlic cloves 
(1/4) cup walnut pieces, lightly toasted
1 small jalapeno, halved and seeded
(1/4) cup freshly grated parmesan or asiago cheese
(1/4) tsp salt
1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 Tbsp lime juice
1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions: 

Place first 6 ingredients in blender or food processor and blend. Slowly add lemon juice, lime juice, and oil, and process until well mixed. Serve, or refrigerate for up to 5 days.
