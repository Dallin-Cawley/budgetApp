                                Enchilada Sauce
                                       
1 can tomato sauce
1 (1/2) c. water
2 T. flour
1 (1/2) t. onion flakes
1 t. salt
(1/2) t. chili powder
(1/4) t. garlic powder
1/8 t. pepper
(1/2) t. paprika
1 T. parmesan cheese

Combine all ingredients except for cheese.  Simmer 10 mintutes.  Add cheese.
