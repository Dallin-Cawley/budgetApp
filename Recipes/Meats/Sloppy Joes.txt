                                  Sloppy Joes
                                       
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger
(1/2)  to 1 c. celery
(1/2) to 1 c. green pepper
(1/2) c. chopped onion
1 small bottle ketchup
1 can tomato soup
3 T. dried mustard
3 T. vinegar
3 T. brown sugar
Instructions: 
Brown hamburger with onion and green pepper.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer until done.
