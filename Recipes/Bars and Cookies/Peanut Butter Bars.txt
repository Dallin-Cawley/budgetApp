                              Peanut Butter Bars
                                       
Ingredients:

(1/2) cup softened butter
(1/2) cup white sugar
(1/2) cup brown sugar
1 egg
(1/3) cup creamy peanut butter
(1/2) tsp soda
(1/4) tsp salt
(1/2) tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1 cup rolled oats

Instructions: 

Cream sugar and butter. Blend in rest of ingredients. Press into 9x13 pan. Bake 15-20 minutes on 350Â°. Sprinkle with chocolate chips or frost with chocolate frosting.
