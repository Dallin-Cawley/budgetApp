                                Corn Casserole
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, cubed 1 inch
2 c. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
(3/4) c. milk
(1/2) t. garlic salt
(1/4) lb. smoked ham, diced (1/4) inch
10 oz. kernel corn, frozen
Instructions: In a four quart saucepan, over low heat, melt cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and milk.  Stir constantly to prevent scorching, until thickened, smooth, and bubbly.  Stir in garlic salt and remove from heat.  Stir in corn and ham.  Transfer mixture to a 2 quart casserole dish.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
