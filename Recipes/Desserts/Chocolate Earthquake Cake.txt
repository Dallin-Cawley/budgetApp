                    Chocolate Earthquake Cake (dutch oven)
Ingredients:
Coconut
Pecans
German chocolate cake mix
Filling:
8 oz. cream cheese
1 c. butter
1 t. vanilla
1 lb. powdered sugar
Instructions: Layer 12 in. dutch oven with coconut and pecans to taste.  Mix cake mix as per box and pour mixture over coconut and pecans.  Cream together cream cheese, butter, vanilla, and powdered sugar.  Using a spoon, plop mixture in cake batter until gone.  Bake with about 6-7 coals on bottom and 18-20 on top for about 30-40 mintues or until bake cracks and shows filling.  Best served warm with ice cream.
