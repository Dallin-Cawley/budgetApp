                              Boiled Raisin Cake
Ingredients:
1 pkg. seeded raisins
2 c. sugar
1 c. shortening
2 t. cinnamon
1 t. nutmeg
(1/2) t. cloves
3 c. water
Instructions:  Boil these ingredients for 10 minutes then cool.  Add 1 t. vanilla.  Put 2 t. soda and 1 t. salt in flour.  Add enough to make quite a stiff dough.  Graham or whole wheat flour can be used.  Variations: 1 lb. gumdrops or any fruit cake mix and/or 1 c. nuts.  Bake slowly for at least one hour or until testing stick comes clean.
