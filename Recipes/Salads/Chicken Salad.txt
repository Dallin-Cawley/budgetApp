                                 Chicken Salad
Ingredients:
2-2 (1/2) c. cubed cooked chicken
(1/4) c. chopped green onions
1 c. thinly sliced celery
1 c. chopped sweet crisp apple, such as gala or honeycrisp
1 c. halved grapes
(1/2) c. mayo
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. cider vinegar
2 T. minced fresh parsley
1 (1/2) t. prepared mustard
(1/2) t. salt
1/8 t. pepper
Instructions: In large bowl, combine chicken, onion, celery, apples, and grapes.  Combine next 7 ingredients; mix well.  Pour over chicken mixture and toss.  Chill for at least 1 hour.
