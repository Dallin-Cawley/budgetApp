                               Glazed Ham Slices
Ingredients:
(1/4) c. packed brown sugar
1 (1/2) t. all-purpose flour
(1/2) t. ground mustard
2 T. ginger ale
1 T. vinegar
2 fully cooked ham slices (1/2-3/4 lb. and (1/2) inch thick)
Instructions: In a skillet combine brown sugar, flour, mustard, ginger ale, and vinegar.  Bring to a boil over low heat; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until sugar is dissolved and sauce is thickened.  Add ham slices and heat through.  Makes 2 servings.
