                      Zucchini Raisin Whole Wheat Muffins
Ingredients:
1 c. all purpose flour
1 c. whole wheat flour
2 t. cinnamon
2 t. baking powder
1/8 ground cloves
(1/2) t. salt
(1/2) c. butter or margarine
(1/2) c. packed brown sugar
2 beaten eggs
 (1/4) c. milk
1 (1/2) c. finely shredded zucchini
(1/2) c. light raisins or currents
Instructions: Mix together dry ingredients in a bowl.  In a separate bowl, beat the butter until soft.  Add brown sugar and beat until light and fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time beating after each addition.  Add milk.  Combine the flour mixture and wet mixture.  Add the zucchini and raisins.  Bake at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
