                              Creamy Rice Pudding
Ingredients:
2 qts. Milk
(1/2) t. salt
1 c. reg. long grain rice
(1/2) c. seedless raisins
(3/4) c. sugar
1 T. vanilla
2-12 oz. cans evaporated milk
4 eggs
Cinnamon
Marashino cherries for garnish
Instructions: Prepare about 2 hours before serving.  In a 4 qt. saucepan over medium heat, heat milk and salt to simmering; stir in rice.  Reduce heat to low cover and simmer 45-50 minutes until rice is very tender and mixture is thick, Stirring occasionally.  Stir in raisins.  Makes about 16 servings.
